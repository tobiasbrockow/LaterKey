Amazing Features

LaterKey let's you decide the timing of your text messages. It's an keyboard app where you can choose the amount of minutes until the text gets send.

Send in ... Minutes

Adjust the time however you want. You can even change to seconds or hours.

Use it Everywhere

It's a keyboard app. It works for services like WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger and SMS.

Edit the Text at any Time

Until it's send, you can always edit the message or even delete it.

Easily Customize

Customize the delay buttons so that you have fast access to your favorite delay time.

Get Notified

You can get notifications like "your text has been send".

Keep the Overview

You can access all your delayed texts in one dashboard and edit them.
